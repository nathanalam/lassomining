Princeton University Lasso Miner

This tool is meant to help identify lasso peptides from sets of genomes

To launch the tool, go to "Status" in the navbar, hit the launch button, add the appropriate accession numbers for the desired genomes, and name your run

You can monitor your run by refreshing the page periodically

Once complete, go to "Matches" and hit the search button. There, you can specify the genomes and the run number desired
